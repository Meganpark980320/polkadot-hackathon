# StableHacks 2026 - Complete Research

## Overview
**Full Name:** StableHacks: Building Institutional Stablecoin Infrastructure on Solana
**Format:** Fully online, global hackathon (10 days)
**Platform:** DoraHacks (https://dorahacks.io/hackathon/stablehacks)
**Cost:** Free
**Focus:** Institutional-grade stablecoin infrastructure on Solana — targeting real-world, scalable use cases at the intersection of DeFi and TradFi, with emphasis on regulatory-aligned, security-first, production-ready solutions.

## Tracks / Categories

Challenge tracks are provided by three organizations:

### 1. Institutional Permissioned DeFi Vaults
- Build regulated, access-controlled DeFi vault infrastructure
- Focus on institutional-grade security and compliance
- Challenge provider: AMINA Bank

### 2. Cross-Border Stablecoin Treasury
- Develop cross-border payment corridors and treasury management systems using stablecoins
- Focus on real-world international settlement use cases
- Challenge provider: Solana Foundation

### 3. Programmable Stablecoin Payments
- Create programmable payment infrastructure using stablecoins
- Focus on automated, conditional, and smart-contract-driven payment flows
- Challenge provider: Solstice Labs

## Rules & Requirements

### Team Requirements
- **Teams only** — solo submissions will NOT be accepted
- Specific team size limits not publicly documented (typical hackathon range: 2-5 members)
- Designed for "experienced developers, startups, and financial innovators"

### Technical Requirements
- All projects **must be built on Solana**
- Must include an **MVP, live prototype, or active/private beta**
- **Concept-only submissions will NOT be considered**
- Solutions must be production-ready, regulatory-aligned, and security-first

### Submission Requirements
- Submit via DoraHacks platform before the deadline (March 23, 5 AM UTC)
- Submissions can be edited until the deadline, after which they are locked for judging
- Late submissions will not be considered

---

## Judging Criteria

Specific judging rubric/weights have not been publicly detailed in available sources. Based on the hackathon's stated focus, projects are expected to be evaluated on:
- **Technical implementation** (working MVP/prototype on Solana)
- **Institutional readiness** (regulatory alignment, compliance, security)
- **Real-world scalability** (production-readiness, practical use case viability)
- **Innovation** (novel approaches to institutional stablecoin challenges)
- **Business viability** (fit for institutional adoption, integration potential)

The jury deliberation happens at the Zurich Demo Day on April 8.
